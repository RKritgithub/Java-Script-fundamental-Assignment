Operators in JavaScript are symbols that perform operations on variables and values. They are essential in programming as they help in executing calculations, comparisons, logical decisions, and more. ### **Types of Operators in JavaScript**: 1. **Arithmetic Operators** (`+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `%`, `++`, `--`) – Perform mathematical operations. 2. **Comparison Operators** (`==`, `===`, `!=`, `<`, `>`, `<=`, `>=`) – Compare values. 3. **Logical Operators** (`&&`, `||`, `!`) – Combine or invert conditions. 4. **Assignment Operators** (`=`, `+=`, `-=`, `*=`, `/=`) – Assign values. 5. **Bitwise Operators** (`&`, `|`, `^`, `~`, `<<`, `>>`) – Work at the binary level. Operators are crucial as they allow JavaScript to handle data dynamically, making programs more functional and interactive.
